Two Assessment Modes

This exam offers flexibility to support your learning:

  • Practice Mode: Work through problems with unlimited attempts to strengthen your understanding and build confidence
  • Official Exam: Your graded assessment covering all Unit 1 geometry topics (let Sparky know when you're ready to begin)

What to Expect

This comprehensive exam will assess your understanding of First Quarter geometry concepts through:

  • Problems drawn randomly from all Unit 1 content areas
  • Visual and conceptual questions requiring sketches and diagrams
  • Discussion-based answers that demonstrate deep understanding
  • An oral review component after the exam to discuss your reasoning

Important: Have your notebook ready for sketching. Use standard keyboard characters for mathematical notation (sqrt(), ^, _). The oral review may affect your final grade.
